About me

In the heart of bustling London, I immersed myself in algorithms, code, and the electric buzz of technology. The city’s tech scene fueled my ambition, but deep down, I craved a connection to nature—a simpler life beyond screens.

Against the urban current, I packed my dreams and headed for the Isle of Skye. Here, connectivity was scarce, isolation profound, and the journey to Edinburgh a challenge. Yet, I adapted. The helicopter masts brought internet lifelines, community bonds eased loneliness, and I learned to cherish Skye’s quiet moments.

Now, in Edinburgh, I stride through university corridors, my laptop humming with code. The resilience forged on Skye propels me—a bridge between worlds. I’m not just a computing student; I’m a testament to dreams flourishing even in the remotest corners.
